About us

Our mission

Splash conducts free learn-to-swim and water safety courses for targeted communities who may not have access or financial resources for swim lessons.

Taught by experienced coaches in a supportive group setting, Splash swimmers learn an important life skill while developing confidence and well-being both in and out of the water.

Our values

Safety:
provide a safe environment for our participants

Professionalism:
meet the highest coaching standards. Carefully encourage participants to succeed, and help some excel

Life skill:
recognise swimming as a life skill that helps develop confidence and self-esteem in and out of the water

Awareness:
respectful, empathetic, and mindful of where people are

Sociable:
create a welcoming environment where friendships between participants flourish and extend beyond the programme

Happiness:
encourage a joy of swimming through passionate and enthusiastic teaching. Make each session varied, fun and fulfilling
