Thank you for your interest in Splash! We run 8 week learn-to-swim courses for targeted communities in Hong Kong. The course is free but participants are expected to commit to all 8 sessions.

Foreign Domestic Workers

Our classes for FDW take place on Sunday afternoons in Tai Tam and Braemar Hill. Our next course for Beginners is tentatively scheduled to start September 11. Registration will open up two weeks prior and the registration link will be posted on our Facebook page so please 'like' our page to stay up to date. Please note you will need to have a valid email address to register. Splash Facebook Page

Employers

All of our coaches volunteer their time, but we do rely on donations to cover pool space rental, insurance and equipment. If you would like to support Splash programmes, please visit our just giving page at https://campaign.justgiving.com/charity/oceanrecov/splash. If your company may be interested in corporate sponsorship opportunities, please contact Edie at edie.hu@gmail.com

Coaches

Are you a coach or experienced swimmer? Come join the Splash team! Schedule and time commitments are flexible and prior coaching experience is not required as we do provide training. To learn more, email Simon yadilloh@hotmail.com or Ria annaria@gmail.com
